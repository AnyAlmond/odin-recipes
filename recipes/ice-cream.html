Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

Vanilla ice cream is a classic summertime dessert. It can be enjoyed in a bowl or on an ice cream cone, or made into a milk shake.

Vanilla is the perfect base flavor to add toppings to. Try topping with chocolate syrup or sliced fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups half-and-half cream
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Whisk heavy cream and half-and-half together in a large bowl.
  2. Slowly whisk sugar into the cream mixture. Once blended, mix the vanilla extract into the mix.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for 3 hours minimum, but up to 3 days.
  4. Whisk to make sure no ingredients settled. Add mix to an ice cream maker and follow manufacturer instructions.
  5. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
