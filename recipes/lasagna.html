Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an italian layered pasta dish that is baked like a casserole. This is a great entree to feed the whole family, or to serve at a dinner party. It also stores well to have for leftovers. You can even make it ahead of time and freeze it until you're ready to have it.

It is popular to serve lasagna with a side salad and/or garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb sean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 lb mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium-high heat on the stove until meat is browned and onions are softened.
  2. Stir crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato pasted into the cooked meat. Season mixture with parsley, dried basil, Italian seasoning, salt and pepper. Add more seasonings to taste. Cover and simmer for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Boil water and cook lasagna noodles according to their package instructions.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  5. To assemble lasagna:
    • Spread a layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish.
    • Arrange noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread ricotta over the noodles.
    • Add roughly 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese over the ricotta.
    • Repeat layering process.
    • Add Parmesan cheese over the top.
    • Cover dish with foil
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 25-30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes.
