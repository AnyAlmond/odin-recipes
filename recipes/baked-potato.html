Baked Potato

This is a simple recipe for a baked potato prepared using an air fryer. Baked potatoes are a good option for the budget conscious. They can be served as either an entree or a side dish.

Baked potatoes also make a great base for lots of other foods, so get creative.

Ingredients

  • Russet potato
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees F
  2. Bush the potatoes with oil and sprinkle with salt. Place in the air fryer basket, and then into the preheated air fryer.
  3. Cook for about 1 hour, or until potatoes pierce easily with a fork. Serve and enjoy.
